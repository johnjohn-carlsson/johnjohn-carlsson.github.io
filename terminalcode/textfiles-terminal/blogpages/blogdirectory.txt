        <h2>01/03 2024 - evenSteven()</h2>
        <h3>Actually useful code?!</h3>
        <p>No, it’t not 1992 even though my program may look like it. I present to you, my way of solving mine and my fiance’s <br>
        bill payments for each month; evenSteven().</p>

        <p>evenSteven() accepts an indefinite amount of *args, however for the sake of the webpage I have limited the program to <br>
        up to four persons for payment calculations. It then calculates the appropriate amount for each person to pay with regards to <br>
        income and how much they have already paid, making things even Steven!</p>

        <p>Javascript has been the bane of my web class, and one I get somewhat used to it I may add some sort of responsive <br>
        persons selector instead of the four empty fiends like this, but for now this 90’s style static page will do just fine.</p>

        <p>As the saying goes; if it works and it’s stupid, it ain’t stupid.</p>
        
        <p>You can try evenSteven() <a href="https://john-john.nu/evensteven/" target="_blank">here</a>!</p>
