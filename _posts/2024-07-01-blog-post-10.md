"The Kunze-method"
======

Hello again!
It's been a while, and thats partly why I'm writing this. I've noticed a pattern when
it comes to what I'll call my hobbies; the lack of consistency.

I consider myself curious and willing to learn, perhaps even a fast learner.
These are good traits and something I value in myself, but letting myself lean into
these often intense and short-sighted focuses all to often may in fact be doing more 
harm than good, you know what they say about too much of a good thing...

In short I've realised that I have a hard time keeping up any sort of consistency 
mainly because I keep shifting my focus, so I've decided to make some changes, mainly
in the form of a schedule.

- I will be submitting content to this website once a week.
- I will be at the very least <i>interacting</i> on LinkedIn twice a week.
- My focus will be to finish projects rather than starting new ones.

Lastly I want to say that this is not some epiphany that randomly stuck me, 
but something I've come to realise about myself after talking to a friend 
that manages to produce an impressive level of consistency and quality when it 
comes to work, hobbies and general life; and that is why I've chosen to call this
the 'Kunze-method'.


So here's to trying new things, even if that means trying less things!
