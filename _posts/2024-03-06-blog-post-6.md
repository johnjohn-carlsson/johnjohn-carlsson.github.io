Actually useful code?!
======

No, it't not 1992 even though my program may look like it.
I present to you, my way of solving mine and my fiance's bill
payments for each month; evenSteven().

evenSteven() accepts an indefinite amount of *args, however
for the sake of the webpage I have limited the program to up to
four persons for payment calculations. It then calculates the 
appropriate amount for each person to pay with regards to income
and how much they have already paid, making things even Steven!

Javascript has been the bane of my web class, and one I get 
somewhat used to it I may add some sort of responsive persons 
selector instead of the four empty fiends like this, but for now
this 90's style static page will do just fine. 

As the saying goes; if it works and it's stupid, it ain't stupid.

You can try evenSteven() [here!](https://johnjohn-carlsson.github.io/evensteven/)
