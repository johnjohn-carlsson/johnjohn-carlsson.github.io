"FlickPick, machinelearning & more"
======


first_year_of_programming_complete = True

One year down, one more to go! <br>
Time has really flown by and I'm really happy with
what I've learned in just one year. We went from writing single script calculators 
to making predictions using artificial neural networks.

Our final assignment was a group assignment where we had to use machinelearning to
find solutions to one of several different tasks, and we chose a movie finder
application that we decided to call "FlickPick". The objective was to find 
suitable movies based on a written input describing what movie the user wanted to see.

In the end we went with a Nearest Neighbor model which was fitted with a vectorized
dataset of keywords from movies to match a vectorized user input, and I'm happy to 
say we got ourselves a working product! <br>
In the world of machinelearning working or not is not so much as relevant as to what
degree of accuracy the model predicts its results, but I think we managed to get
somewhat accurate results. Most importantly we learned a ton and had I had a blast!

Next year we will be going deeper into applied AI in programming and a majority of 
the semester will be allocated for internships. It's going to be really interesting
to get a taste of some real world programming, but until then I have 9 weeks of
summer vacation to spend with my friends and family. Life is good.
